title: Don't forget about the people chasing you
---

While running the local Turkey Trot 10k I couldn't help but notice the faster runners passing me by during the race. But what I didn't think about was the __slower__ runners behind me that I'd never even get to see. 

It reminds me how we tend to look at life. We're always focusing on who's better, more successful, or more famous than ourselves. But we hardly ever reflect on those we've passed along the way. There will always be people who are better than us, and comparing ourselves to them is a losing endeavor. We need to balance these thoughts with remembering all those we passed along the way and how much they might wish they were in our shoes. 

_Side note: ended up finishing the 10k in 1:05 (the course was 100% trails). Getting old sucks!_
